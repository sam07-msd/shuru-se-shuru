For Arpiii

❤️

To the girl
who once made
ordinary days
feel special.

Agar Shuru Se Shuru Karun...

Some stories deserve one more beginning.

Agar shuru se shuru karun...

Agar shuru se shuru karun,
Toh iss baar mohabbat ho jaayegi kya?
Tumhare pasandida kaam karun,
Toh tum meri ho paogi kya?
Ek shaam aaun tumhare shehar,
Toh mere saath coffee pe chal paogi kya?
Maangna hai Bhagwan se tumko,
Toh mere saath Jagannath Mandir mein pooja kar paogi kya?
Laal chudiyan dekar tumhe manaun,
Toh unhe pehenkar itraogi kya?
Kuch baat kehni hai tumse,
Toh thodi der thehar jaogi kya?

Agar shuru se shuru karun,
Toh iss baar mohabbat ho jaayegi kya...?

♥

Agar shuru se shuru karun,
Mujhe apni aadat bana paogi kya?
Aawaaz pasand hai tumhari,
Apne din kaise guzre, har din suna paogi kya?
Abhi rastein bahat badi si hai, un raston ke upar..
Wahan kuch lamhe sirf mere saath bita paogi kya?
Apni hi baaton mein uljha rehta hoon aksar,
Unhe samajhkar suljha paogi kya?

♥

Agar shuru se shuru karun,
Toh tum ruk jaogi kya?
Aadat nahi hai mujhe kisi ke saath rehne ki,
Meri buri aadatein badal paogi kya?
Gusse mein kabhi tumhe chale jaane ko bol doon,
Toh sach mein chhod kar jaogi kya?
Rooth jaata hoon chhoti si baaton par,
Har baar mana paogi kya?
Agar tumhare saamne ro padun kabhi,
Toh meri khamoshi bhi samajh paogi kya?
Jin baaton se mujhe darr lagta hai,
Unhe bina kahe yaad rakh paogi kya?
Humesha baatein kehte-kehte ruk jaata hoon,
Tum meri an-kahi baatein bhi sun paogi kya?

♥

Agar shuru se shuru karun,
Toh mujhe kahaniyon wala pyaar kar paogi kya?
Mujhe roothna hoga tumse kabhi,
Toh tum manaane aaogi kya?
Main bhaagna chahta hoon khud se kabhi,
Toh mujhe dhoondhne aaogi kya?
Kuch suljha raha hoon zindagi mein,
Thoda intezaar kar paogi kya?
Agar kabhi sambhal na paun khud ko,
Toh mera haath thaam kar saath nibha dogi kya?

♥

Chalo maan lo... shuru se shuru kar hi liya.
Phir bhi...
Humari adhuri kahaani poori kar paogi kya?
Jo ghaav dil par waqt ne diye hain,
Unpar apne pyaar ka marham laga paogi kya?
Agar phir se tumhare paas aaun,
Toh humare beech ki daraarein mita paogi kya?
Himmat karke apna toota hua dil leke
Aaj phir tumhare hawaale kar raha hoon...
Is baar...
Use tutne se bacha paogi kya?
Aur agar toot hi gaya kahin,
Toh apne haathon se jod paogi kya?

Bas ek aakhri sawaal...

Agar shuru se shuru karun...

Toh iss baar, sirf mohabbat nahi...

Zindagi bhar mera saath nibha paogi kya?

❤️

— Satyam
